Table 1: Questionnaires Used for Data Collection

Variable Sample Question Scale
Perceived Ease of Use (PEU) I think the charging process of electric vehicles is simple and easy to operate. 1-5 Likert Scale
(1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree)
I think the daily use and maintenance of electric vehicles are convenient.
Number of Charging Piles (NCP) There are enough charging piles near my home for electric vehicle use. 1-5 Likert Scale
(1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree)
I think the number of charging piles can meet my daily needs.
After-Sales Maintenance Distance (AMD) The after-sales maintenance service for electric vehicles is located close to my home. 1-5 Likert Scale
(1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree)
I think the convenience of after-sales maintenance for electric vehicles is high.
Attitude (ATT) I think purchasing an electric vehicle has a positive impact on environmental protection. 1-5 Likert Scale
(1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree)
I have a positive attitude toward the experience of using electric vehicles.
Cold Winter Performance (CWP) I think electric vehicles perform well in the cold winters of Northeast China. 1-5 Likert Scale
(1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree)
I believe electric vehicles can operate normally in low-temperature environments.
Advertising (ADV) Advertisements for electric vehicles make me feel positive about them. 1-5 Likert Scale
(1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree)
I think the content of electric vehicle advertisements is truthful and credible.
Policy Guidance (PG) Government subsidies for electric vehicles make me more willing to purchase one. 1-5 Likert Scale
(1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree)
I think government policies effectively promote the adoption of electric vehicles.
Subjective Norm (SN) My family and friends support my decision to purchase an electric vehicle. 1-5 Likert Scale
(1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree)
People around me think purchasing an electric vehicle is a good choice.
Perceived Behavioral Control (PBC) I have sufficient resources and ability to purchase and use an electric vehicle. 1-5 Likert Scale
(1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree)
I think I can easily handle the use and maintenance of an electric vehicle.
Maintenance Frequency (MF) I think the maintenance frequency of electric vehicles is acceptable. 1-5 Likert Scale
(1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree)
I believe the maintenance of electric vehicles will not take up too much of my time.
Perceived Usefulness (PU) I think electric vehicles can save costs in the long run. 1-5 Likert Scale
(1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree)
I think using an electric vehicle can improve my quality of life.
Vehicle Price (VP) I think the price of electric vehicles is within my budget. 1-5 Likert Scale
(1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree)
I think the price of electric vehicles matches their performance.
Brand Effect (BE) I prefer to purchase electric vehicles from well-known brands. 1-5 Likert Scale
(1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree)
I think electric vehicles from well-known brands are more reliable.
Purchase Intention (PI) I plan to purchase an electric vehicle in the future. 1-5 Likert Scale
(1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree)
I am likely to purchase an electric vehicle in the near future.
